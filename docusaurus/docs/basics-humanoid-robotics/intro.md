# Chapter 2: Basics of Humanoid Robotics

Humanoid robots are robots with human-like features and capabilities. They are designed to mimic human appearance and behavior, making them ideal for human-robot interaction and tasks that require human-like dexterity and mobility.

## Anatomy of Humanoid Robots

Humanoid robots typically feature:

- **Bipedal locomotion**: Two legs for walking
- **Upper body**: Arms and hands for manipulation
- **Head**: With sensors for perception
- **Torso**: Connecting all components

## Key Technologies

- **Actuators**: Motors and servos for movement
- **Sensors**: Cameras, microphones, touch sensors
- **Control systems**: Algorithms for balance and coordination
- **AI systems**: For perception and decision making

## Applications

Humanoid robots are used in:

- Healthcare assistance
- Customer service
- Education
- Research
- Entertainment
